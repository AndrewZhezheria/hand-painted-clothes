    HOW TO CARE FOR PAINTED CLOTHES

    • 1. Delicate washing mode (no more than 30 degrees).
    • 2. Minimal spin and no bleach.
    • 3. It is important to turn the garment inside out before washing.
    • 4. Do not touch the pattern with an iron.
    • 5. Do not bend the image.
